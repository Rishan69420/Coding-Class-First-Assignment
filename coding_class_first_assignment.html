Squirrel





Squirrels don't dig up all of their burried
nuts, which results in more trees! They
have accidentally contributed countless trees to our nation's forests.

Scientific Name: Sciuridae
Average Length: 7.1 to 9.8 Inches
Average Lifespan: 6 to 10 years
Habitat: Hardwood/ Mixed Deciduous Forests, Urban and Rural Areas.

Squirrels are native to the eastern part of the
United States and southeastern Canada, with
a range stretching from Quebec south to
Florida and west into the eastern third of
Texas. Squirrels can be found outside of this
range, however, as they have been introduced
into Oregon, Washington, California, Montana
and British Columbia.
